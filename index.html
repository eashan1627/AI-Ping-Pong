AI Ping Pong Game











Developed By: Eashan Singh

Instructions

1. First keep your laptop screen straight.

2. Move yourself approximatly 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

3. Move your wrist in an acending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist

4. Now press the play button and place your self 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your nose.
